ERROR CLASS 2: Product / Brand Retrieved Instead of Parent Company
==================================================================

EXPLANATION:
Every NAME THE PARENT COMPANY question except one (Fisher-Price → Mattel) is wrong.
The clue is simply the name of a product or brand. BM25 retrieves the Wikipedia article
about the product itself — which is prominent and has strong vocabulary overlap with the
query — rather than the corporate parent's article. The parent company article mentions
the product name less frequently and scores lower as a result. A targeted fix would be
to detect this category and reformulate the query to search for "subsidiary of" or
"owned by" relationships, or to post-filter candidates by whether they describe a
company rather than a product.

  Gold     : Kraft Foods
  Predicted: Jell-O
  Note     : BM25 returns the Jell-O article directly. Kraft Foods scores lower
             because "Jell-O" is one of many brands mentioned there.
