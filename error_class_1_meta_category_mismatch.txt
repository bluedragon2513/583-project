ERROR CLASS 1: Meta-Category Answer Domain Mismatch
=====================================================

QUESTIONS IN CLASS: 10
CATEGORIES AFFECTED: STATE OF THE ART MUSEUM, CAPITAL CITY CHURCHES, COMPLETE DOM-INATION

EXPLANATION:
Some categories embed a game rule that completely redirects the answer domain. BM25
retrieves documents similar to the clue text, but the correct answer belongs to a
*different* domain that the clue never mentions. No amount of query tuning can fix
this class — the right article is simply not in the top-10 candidates.

  - STATE OF THE ART MUSEUM: "We'll give you the museum. You give us the state."
    BM25 retrieves museum articles; U.S. state articles never appear.
  - CAPITAL CITY CHURCHES: "We'll give you the church. You tell us the capital city."
    BM25 retrieves church/figure articles; the capital city article is absent.
  - COMPLETE DOM-INATION: Every answer must contain the substring "dome."
    BM25 has no substring filter; it retrieves topically relevant articles regardless
    of whether "dome" appears in their title.

The fix requires a pre-retrieval rewrite: for museum→state, search which state's
Wikipedia article mentions the named museum; for church→city, look up the church
article and extract its location metadata; for the dome constraint, apply a title
substring filter after retrieval.
